# Integral Formulas ### 1. Basic Integral Formula The integral of a function $f(x)$ over $x$ is represented as: $$ \int f(x) \, dx $$ ### 2. Power Rule for Integration For any real number $n \neq -1$: $$ \int x^n \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C $$ ### 3. Integral of Exponential Functions The integral of the exponential function $e^x$ is: $$ \int e^x \, dx = e^x + C $$ ### 4. Integral of Trigonometric Functions #### Sine Function $$ \int \sin(x) \, dx = -\cos(x) + C $$ #### Cosine Function $$ \int \cos(x) \, dx = \sin(x) + C $$ #### Secant-Squared Function $$ \int \sec^2(x) \, dx = \tan(x) + C $$ #### Cosecant-Squared Function $$ \int \csc^2(x) \, dx = -\cot(x) + C $$ ### 5. Integral of Rational Functions The integral of $\frac{1}{x}$: $$ \int \frac{1}{x} \, dx = \ln |x| + C $$ ### 6. Definite Integral Example For example, the definite integral of $x^2$ from $0$ to $1$ is: $$ \int_0^1 x^2 \, dx = \frac{1}{3} $$ ### 7. Fundamental Theorem of Calculus If $F(x)$ is the antiderivative of $f(x)$ on $[a, b]$, then: $$ \int_a^b f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a) $$ ### 8. Double Integral The double integral of $f(x, y)$ over a region $R$ is written as: $$ \iint_R f(x, y) \, dA $$ ### 9. Triple Integral For a function $f(x, y, z)$ over a volume $V$: $$ \iiint_V f(x, y, z) \, dV $$ ### 10. Surface Integral The surface integral of a scalar field $\phi(x, y, z)$ over a surface $S$ is: $$ \iint_S \phi(x, y, z) \, dS $$
